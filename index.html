I am a passionate game programmer from Valencia, Spain. I started coding back in 2020 when I started my university journey at ESAT in Valencia studying a HND in computing. After doing three years at ESAT, I got my HND and decided to come to Sheffield to finish my studies and to get a Bachelor degree in computer science for games. Along the way I learned a lot of tech and gain experience with multiple tools that are commonly used in the video games industry.

As for the programming language that I have more experience with, it is C and C++ although I also have some experience with C# from working with Unity. In terms of third party engines, I specialize in Unreal Engine 4 and 5 having plenty of experience, including a game published in Steam. On top of that I also have plenty of experience using Unity. Regarding graphic APIs the ones I have the most experience with are OpenGL, GLSL and the PlayStation®5 API. On top of that I have also some experience with DirectX12. On top of that I am also very experienced using ImGui due to that I have used it in most of my projects. Finally, I have a strong understanding of source control tools such as GitHub, Perforce, Bitbucket and Unity Version Control.

Although I have knowledge and experience in many areas, the tasks and projects that I enjoy the most working on are those that include gameplay and AI as the main focus. Gameplay related, I have worked on many projects that required such kind of work with both first and third party engines. As for AI it has always been a topic of my interest. I have a bunch of experience developing AI for NPCs in Unreal Engine, I have also worked with machine learning and procedural generation techniques on Unity and my Bachelor dissertation is about Wave Function Collapse, a procedural generation algorithm which I personalized to be able to generate chunks of terrain. This last project was built on a custom built engine from my year previous to the bachelor.
