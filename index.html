I am a passionate programmer from Valencia, Spain. I started coding back in 2020 when I started my university journey at ESAT in Valencia studying a HND in computing. After completing my HND with an average grade of distinction, I decided to move to Sheffield to finish my studies. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in computer science for games, with first class honours, at Sheffield Hallam University. Along the way I learned a lot of tech and gain experience with multiple tools.

I have experience working with the following programming languages: C, C++, Python, C#, Kotlin and Java. Regarding game engines, I specialize in Unreal Engine 4 and 5 which I have used in plenty of projects, including a game published in Steam. On top of that I also have plenty of experience using Unity. Regarding graphic APIs, the ones I have the most experience with are: OpenGL and the PlayStation®5 API. On top of that, I have basic understanding of DirectX12. I am also very experienced using ImGui since I have used it in most of my projects. Finally, I have a strong understanding of source control tools such as GitHub, Perforce, Bitbucket and PlasticSCM.
